About FullStack Web Development

FullStack Development refers to the developmnet of both front-end and back-end portions of an application. This web development process involves alll three layer- Presentation layer(front-end user interface), Business Logic Layer(back-end with data validation)and the database layer.

Hence a fullstack developer can develop strategies for every part of theweb development process. He/She develops this deep knowledge of the systems throgh years of experiencein working in this field.
So a fullstack developer is nothing less than a veteran who has high resposibilities.Such skillsare difficult to gather and people who possess these skills are greatly in-demand in the industry.
